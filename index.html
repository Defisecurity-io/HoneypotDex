How does it work?

Connect your wallet and our smart contract will scan for any eligible tokens. If you have any eligible tokens in your wallet our smart contract will detect them and reverse the original buy transaction so there will be a transaction fee involved in order to receive the original amount of ETH or chain specific token in return instantly. If your eligible tokens are found on different chains like Arbitrum, Optimism or Polygon you will be prompted to switch to those chains. That's all. It works on most networks.
